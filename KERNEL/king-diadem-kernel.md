# KING DIADEM Kernel

Core governance rules for preventing **Choice Collapse**.

---

## Core Principle

Systems collapse when choices disappear.

Governance exists to prevent that moment.

---

## System Invariant

Valid Option ≥ 1

A system remains survivable as long as at least one viable option exists.

Collapse occurs when:

Choice → 0

---

## Governance Rules

Ego OFF  
Evidence ON

Authority must be supported by verifiable evidence.

Stop-the-Line Authority  
Any operator may halt a process when harm is detected.

Stabilize before Optimize.

---

## Objective

Detect structural signals of **Choice Collapse** early enough to restore at least one viable option.

Fail Less  
Harm Less  
Restore Choice
