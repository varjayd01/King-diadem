KING DIADEM

Human Survival Scenarios

Purpose:
Demonstrate how the system generates multiple viable choices for real-world situations.

---

Scenario 1

Country: Thailand
Resources:
Rice
Water
Mobile Phone
Low Money

Situation:
User is low on money and food supply.

System Output:

Option 1
Preserve resources and reduce consumption.

Option 2
Seek nearby community food support.

Option 3
Offer labor exchange for food or shelter.

Option 4
Travel to a nearby location with better opportunities.

---

Scenario 2

Country: Brazil
Resources:
Water
Basic tools
Limited energy

Situation:
User is stranded with limited supplies.

System Output:

Option 1
Conserve energy and stabilize resources.

Option 2
Signal for assistance.

Option 3
Search nearby area for water or shelter.

Option 4
Attempt relocation toward populated zones.

---

System Principle

The system never forces a decision.

It reveals survivable paths.

Human freedom is preserved.
